💬 FutureCraft – Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What services do you offer?

I offer web design and development, responsive design, landing pages, website maintenance, portfolio and business sites, and website optimization for speed and SEO.

❓ How much do you charge for a website?

Pricing depends on your needs — a simple landing page starts lower than a full business site or e-commerce solution. Contact me for a free quote tailored to your project.

❓ How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites take between 1–3 weeks depending on the complexity, content, and feedback speed. I’ll give you a clear timeline once we’ve discussed your project.

❓ Do you build mobile-friendly websites?

Absolutely. Every site I build is fully responsive and looks great on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

❓ Will I be able to update my website after it's built?

Yes — I can build it with an easy-to-use system (like WordPress) or provide ongoing maintenance if you'd prefer me to manage updates for you.

❓ Do you provide hosting or domain names?

I don’t resell hosting, but I’ll help you choose the best hosting provider and guide you through domain registration if needed.

❓ Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes! I offer redesign services to modernize your site and improve performance, user experience, or branding.
